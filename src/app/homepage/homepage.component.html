Hello everyone. We are DoorHinge apps, a pretty awesome software company. We mainly work with iOS, iPadOS, and macOS app development. Our other projects are related to website building and Android development.

Our Apps

Aura Browser

Aura is a web browser designed for iPad with additional support for iPhone, Mac, and Vision Pro. The app is designed as a customizable and open source alternative to Arc browser. The browser is currently available in beta. You can view the code and contribute on GitHub.

DH Chat

DH Chat is a messaging platform for groups. Our focus with this app was to create a more powerful alternative to GroupMe that maintains simplicity. It is essentially in between GroupMe and Discord. It is not available yet. When it is released, it will support iOS, Android, and Web.
